How It Works

Our POC Engagement Model is designed to build your confidence and independence. You'll start with high-touch support and progressively gain autonomy as you learn. By the end, you'll have shipped a production-ready data product and created reusable patterns for your next one.

Support Model

We start with pair-programming and daily check-ins (High-Touch), transition to office hours and code reviews (Medium-Touch), and end with you working independently with on-call support (Low-Touch). Our goal is to build your confidence, not create dependency.
